Are you a passionate and qualified foreign language teacher ready to embark on a rewarding educational journey? The Ministry of Preschool and School Education in Uzbekistan invites you to be a part of our groundbreaking project, shaping the future of foreign language teaching in our vibrant country!

 

About the Project:

In our mission to elevate the standard of foreign language education, we are launching a project to attract 500 enthusiastic and qualified native-speaking foreign language teachers for the upcoming 2023/2024 academic year. This is your chance to make a significant impact, contribute to global education, and immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of Uzbekistan.

Why Choose Uzbekistan?

  • Cultural Richness: Explore a country with a fascinating history, diverse traditions, and warm hospitality.

  • Global Impact: Play a pivotal role in enhancing language education and fostering cross-cultural understanding.

  • Professional Growth: Join a dynamic teaching environment and gain valuable international teaching experience.

What We Offer:

  • Competitive Packages: We offer attractive remuneration packages along with additional benefits.

  • Comprehensive Support: From visa assistance to housing support, we ensure a smooth transition to life in Uzbekistan.

  • Innovative Teaching: Be a part of a forward-thinking educational system, utilizing modern teaching methodologies.

How to Join:

  1. Submit Your CV: Share your qualifications and teaching experience by submitting your CV.

  2. Showcase Your Skills: Provide a recorded video lesson or a link to a YouTube video demonstrating your teaching prowess.

  3. Schedule a Zoom Interview: Choose a convenient date and time through our Calendly link for a personalized Zoom interview.

Be a Catalyst for Change: By joining our project, you become a catalyst for positive change in the lives of Uzbek students. Your expertise will shape the next generation of global citizens, fostering language proficiency and cultural awareness.
